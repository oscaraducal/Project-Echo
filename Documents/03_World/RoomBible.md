# Room Bible

Status: Active

Version: 0.1

---

# Purpose

Defines standards for room design.

Every room should answer three questions.

---

# 1. What was this room used for?

Every room must have a believable function.

---

# 2. What happened here?

Environmental storytelling should communicate recent history.

Examples:

- Signs of evacuation
- Failed experiments
- Maintenance work
- Violence
- Emergency response

---

# 3. Why is the player here?

The room must contribute to:

- Gameplay
- Story
- Atmosphere

Ideally all three.

---

# Room Checklist

Each room should contain:

- Primary gameplay purpose
- Environmental storytelling
- Lighting plan
- Ambient audio
- Props
- Navigation cues
- Optional lore
