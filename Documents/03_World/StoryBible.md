# Story Bible

## Setting

Asterion Research Institute

An abandoned research facility where Project Echo was conducted.

---

## Core Mystery

Something happened inside the institute.

The player gradually discovers the truth through environmental storytelling, notes, and exploration.

---

## The Witness

Status:

Under Development

Purpose:

Primary psychological threat.

The Witness should create uncertainty rather than constant pursuit.

---

## Storytelling Style

Story is delivered through:

- Notes
- Environmental clues
- Audio logs
- Room design
- Visual storytelling

Avoid long exposition sequences.
