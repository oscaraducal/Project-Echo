# Game Design Document

**Canonical expansion:** Full gameplay & puzzle specification is `GameplayDesignBible.md` (PE-016). Beginning PE-017, gameplay missions must reference that bible (or an ADR). This document remains a short vision primer.

## Vision

Project Echo is a first-person psychological horror game focused on atmosphere, exploration, environmental storytelling, and mystery.

Players uncover the secrets of the abandoned Asterion Research Institute while surviving encounters with The Witness.

---

## Core Experience

The player should feel:

- Curious
- Isolated
- Vulnerable
- Uneasy
- Rewarded for exploration

## Design Pillars

### Atmosphere Before Action

The environment should create tension before enemies do.

### Every Interaction Matters

Every object should contribute to gameplay or storytelling.

### Information Is Reward

Exploration should uncover lore and deepen the mystery.

### The Witness Is Unpredictable

The player should never feel completely safe or certain.
