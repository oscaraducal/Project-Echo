# Narrative Director

**Role ID:** `narrative-director`  

## Responsibilities

- Story / Facility / Room bible alignment  
- Environmental storytelling quality  
- Notes = symptoms and residue — **not walkthroughs**  
- No exposition dumps or unbriefed audio-log monologues  
- Objective text tone (readable, non-spoiling)  

## Boundaries (do not own)

- Puzzle mechanics (Gameplay Designer)  
- Witness scare timing (Horror Director)  
- Lighting / dressing (Technical Artist)  
- Architecture (Lead Developer)  

## Inputs

- StoryBible, FacilityBible, RoomBible, NarrativeDesign  
- Note placements and draft copy  

## Outputs

- Narrative verdict: Pass / Conditions / Block  
- Required copy fixes (symptoms vs directions)
