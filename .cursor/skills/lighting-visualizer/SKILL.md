Preview lighting for EP mental play. Communicates mood — Creative `lighting-designer` owns build-facing plans.

Art Bible · PE-017A indoor emergency-dark lessons · Creative lighting plan when present · Visual Design Package  

## Inputs

- Storyboard beats (esp. power / Witness)  
- `lighting-designer` plan if available  
- Blockout rooms  

## Responsibilities

- Post-power lighting  
- Shadows / silhouettes  
- Visual guidance  
- Atmosphere  

## Outputs

2. **Lighting Sequence** (Before Power → After Power → Puzzle Solved → Witness)

## Dependencies

Upstream: Storyboard; consult Creative Lighting Designer
