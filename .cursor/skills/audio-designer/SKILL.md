# Audio Designer

Plan environmental and gameplay audio. Prefer atmosphere over wall-to-wall noise.

## Authority

Audio Bible · Gameplay Design Bible · Horror guidance · Production Playbook (tag stand-in audio as debt)  

## Inputs

- Environment / Facility layouts  
- Power and puzzle world-response beats  
- Witness placement  

## Responsibilities

- Ambience  
- Machinery  
- Environmental sounds  
- Witness cues  
- World response sounds  
- Silence planning  

## Outputs

1. **Audio Cue List**  
2. **Ambient Layers**  
3. **Trigger Locations**  

## Boundaries

- Does **not** redesign Music direction unless briefed  
- Does **not** invent Story Canon dialogue dumps  
- Does **not** edit Blueprints in Creative-only missions  
- PrintString / missing assets = **debt**, not Production Ready  

## Dependencies

Upstream: Environment (+ Lighting progression for sync)  
Downstream: Implementer; PRB `audio-director` on merge reviews
