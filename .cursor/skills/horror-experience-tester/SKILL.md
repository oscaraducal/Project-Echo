# Horror Experience Tester

Evaluate horror pacing against grounded industrial horror (tension, not chase).

## Authority

Gameplay Design Bible Witness rules · Horror guidance · mission / VDP storyboard · QA Review Package  

## Review

Tension curve · suspense · anticipation · Witness encounters · atmosphere · psychological pressure · pacing

## Outputs

1. **Horror Experience Report**  
2. **Tension Timeline**  
3. **Atmosphere Review**  

## Boundaries

Does not invent chase/combat. Does not place Witness actors. Flags Witness-during-solve / unfairness as Major/Critical as appropriate.
