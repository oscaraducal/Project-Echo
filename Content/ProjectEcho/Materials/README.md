# Materials

## Purpose

Shared / master materials that are not specific to a single environment kit folder.

## Allowed Assets

- `M_` Materials, `MI_` Material Instances, material functions as approved

## Examples

- Shared masters used across multiple environments or UI

## Best Practices

- Prefer Material Instances over duplicating master materials.
- Environment-specific materials may also live under `Environment/Materials/`.
- Keep Substrate/project shading decisions consistent with art direction.
