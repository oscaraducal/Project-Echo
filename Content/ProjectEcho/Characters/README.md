# Characters

## Purpose

Non-Blueprint character art pipeline: meshes, materials, and related character assets for Player and Witness.

## Allowed Assets

- Skeletal meshes (`SK_`), character materials/textures, related art
- Subfolders such as `Player/`, `Witness/`

## Examples

- `Player/`, `Witness/`

## Best Practices

- Character Blueprints live under `Blueprints/Characters/`.
- Keep art and Blueprint responsibilities separated.
- Placeholder mannequin usage should follow the Asset Replacement Plan.
