Visual effects and post-process related content for atmosphere and feedback.

- Prefer Niagara for new effects.
- Do not rename `Niagra` until a dedicated cleanup mission is approved.
- Keep gameplay triggers in Blueprints/Gameplay; FX assets stay presentation-focused.
- Heavy effects should be budgeted for horror atmosphere and target hardware.
